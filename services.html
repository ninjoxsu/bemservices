Aircon & Airduct Cleaning

Clean air is essential to life. Many times we are breathing contaminated air in our homes, offices and industrial sites without even knowing it. The need for cleaning depends on a variety of factors like recent renovations, local weather conditions, smoking and overall cleanliness of the surroundings. Contaminants like dust, construction debris, chunks of drywall and sometimes rodents and mildew accumulate inside the ducts thus necessitate the cleaning of the system.

The result would be the circulation of improved air quality which is beneficial to the health condition of the occupants/tenants of the building, industrial site or even residential houses. Aside from this, the utility charges would be minimized because the efficiency of the air conditioning system has been maximized to the fullest.

The standard of air duct system cleaning followed by BEM Industrial and Janitorial Services complies with the worldwide used standard of NADCA (National Air Duct Cleaners Association).
